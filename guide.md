## How to get here 

#### By train 

There is a suburban train service between Porto and Guimar達es. The service runs usually on a hourly basis and takes between 1h and 1h20 minutes depending on the train. For more information on the schedule, consult the [CP website](https://www.cp.pt/StaticFiles/horarios/urbanos-porto/comboios-urbanos-porto-guimaraes.pdf).

#### By Bus 

There are regular bus services from all regions of Portugal to the central bus station in Guimar達es.
